Instituto Lizardi
Elementary school

Sistema UNOi educational program

UNOi is a digital platform that has all the contents from preschool, elementary and middle school. The students and teachers work with iPads and classrooms are equipped with Apppletv projectors. Thanks to being part of UNOi for over 6 years, we have been able to continue our classes and keep in touch with or students throughout the pandemic.

It has the following characteristics: digital books, agenda, key, audios, videos, files for each subject, and direct access to online classes. Being part of UNOi has allowed us to continue our classes during the contingency because our teachers and students are accustomed to working in a digital environment.

Tools

Our students take advantage of technology guided by pedagogy. Based on the results of our diagnostic evaluations, teachers learn about their characteristics and intellectual potential

Teachers

Our teachers are committed to teaching with a sense pf adaptation and learning with communication and interaction skills and creativity.

Innovation

The Maker environment allows the students to perceive themselves as people who are able to change the world while they develop the necessary skills for their present and future life.

Services and facilities

Our school offers classrooms with audiovisual equipment and internet. One classroom with an interactive board, a “maker classroom” equipped with a 3D printer, arts area, psychological department, library, auditorium for 250 people, cafeteria, and sports courts.
