Instituto Lizardi
Pre-school

What is pre-school?

Our integral program covers cognitive, emotional, and motor areas. The education is designed to develop skills and attitudes that will help the students in their everyday life.

Sistema UNOi

Combines different methodologies such as Montessori, Waldorf, High Scope and Pedagogic inspirations like Project Zero. Its main tools being play, the body and storytelling combined with values and mindfulness.

Bilingual pre-school

Working with Sistema UNOi, we offer our students a natural approach with adequate methodologies and techniques.

School hours

The children attend school from 8:00 a.m. to 2:30 p.m.

Art

We offer painting, music, dance and/or theater to promote the individual development.

Emotional education

It teaches students to control and manage their emotions. Our psychology department works together with teachers, parents, and students.
